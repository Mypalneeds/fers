Our Story

Building Excellence Since 2015

FERS (formerly ECS Retail) was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide Nigerian businesses with world-class retail equipment and solutions that drive success. What started as a small venture has grown into one of Nigeria's most trusted names in retail solutions.

Over the years, we've equipped hundreds of supermarkets, restaurants, bakeries, and commercial establishments across Nigeria. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has remained unwavering.

Premium quality equipment from trusted global brands
Expert installation and comprehensive after-sales support
Customized solutions tailored to your unique needs

Our Mission

To empower Nigerian businesses with premium retail solutions that enhance operational efficiency, improve customer experience, and drive sustainable growth. We are committed to delivering excellence in every project, building lasting partnerships, and setting industry standards.

Our Vision

To be recognized as West Africa's leading provider of innovative retail solutions, known for our unwavering commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and technological advancement. We envision a future where every retail space in Nigeria operates at peak efficiency.
